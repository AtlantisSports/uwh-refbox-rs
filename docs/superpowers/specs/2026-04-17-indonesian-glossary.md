Also confirmed silent communication underwater, 6 active + 4 substitute structure.

**Note on POSSI rulebook:** PB POSSI (Persatuan Olahraga Selam Seluruh Indonesia) is confirmed as the governing body for underwater hockey in Indonesia, but no publicly accessible Indonesian-language UWH rulebook PDF was found during this research. The sport's primary international rulebook is the CMAS Rules of Play (English only). All Indonesian UWH-specific terms below are therefore drawn from club pages, Wikipedia, and Indonesian sports media — not from a primary federation rulebook.
