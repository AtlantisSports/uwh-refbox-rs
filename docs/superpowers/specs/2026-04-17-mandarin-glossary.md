# Mandarin Chinese (Simplified) UWH Glossary — First Pass

**Date:** 2026-04-17
**Locale:** zh-CN (Simplified, Mainland)
**Purpose:** Vet UWH-specific Mandarin terms before generating zh-CN/refbox.ftl.
**Review status:** Pending human review. Native-speaker review and verification by a Chinese UWH
player or coach is a separate, later step.

> **Caveat:** Mainland Chinese has no established UWH federation rulebook in Chinese. The sport
> is played under CMAS international rules (English). Chinese-language UWH documentation is
> sparse: the main sources are Chinese Wikipedia (水底曲棍球), Baidu Baike (水下曲棍球), a
> Hong Kong UWH association intro document (Traditional Chinese), and parallel terminology from
> water polo (水球) and ice hockey (冰球), which share the pool/rink context. Where no UWH-
> specific Chinese term exists, this glossary proposes terms from these parallel sports or from
> general Mandarin sports vocabulary, clearly tagged. All Traditional Chinese variants noted
> where found.
